Most Popular

CPR/AED Training

Learn life-saving techniques with our comprehensive CPR and AED training program.

Our CPR/AED training follows the latest HSI/AHA guidelines and teaches participants how to recognize cardiac emergencies and properly perform CPR for adults, children, and infants. You'll also learn how to use an Automated External Defibrillator (AED) with confidence.

What You'll Learn:
  • How to recognize the signs of cardiac arrest
  • Proper CPR techniques for all age groups
  • AED operation and maintenance
  • Relief of choking
  • How to work as part of a resuscitation team

Duration: 2-3 hours

Certification: Valid for 2 years

Schedule Training

First Aid Training

Comprehensive first aid training for emergency situations.

Our First Aid training equips participants with the knowledge and skills to provide basic first aid in emergency situations. From treating minor cuts to managing serious injuries, our hands-on training prepares you to respond confidently and effectively.

What You'll Learn:
  • Assessment of emergency situations
  • Treatment for bleeding and shock
  • Management of burns and fractures
  • Response to sudden illnesses
  • Recognition and treatment of heat/cold emergencies

Duration: 4-6 hours

Certification: Valid for 2 years

Schedule Training

Combined CPR/First Aid Training

Complete emergency response training in one comprehensive program.

Our combined CPR/First Aid training provides you with all the skills from both our CPR/AED and First Aid programs in one convenient course. This comprehensive training is ideal for workplace requirements, childcare providers, coaches, and anyone wanting to be fully prepared for emergencies.

What You'll Learn:
  • All components of CPR/AED training
  • All components of First Aid training
  • Integrated emergency response techniques

Duration: 6-8 hours (can be split into multiple sessions)

Certification: Valid for 2 years

We specialize in providing tailored training programs for businesses and organizations of all sizes. Our corporate training can be scheduled at your convenience and customized to address the specific needs and potential emergencies in your workplace.
